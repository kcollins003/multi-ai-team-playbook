## Success Criteria

- [What does useful output look like? Be specific.]
- [What decisions does it help you make?]
- [What evidence or data do you need to see?]

A poor result:
- [What are you NOT looking for?]
- [What would waste your time?]

Example:
A good result:
- Identifies real demand from real buyers, not theoretical interest
- Flags competitive products with honest strengths and weaknesses
- Surfaces pricing signals from comparable products
- Gives me enough to make a confident go/no-go decision

A poor result:
- Generic overviews with no actionable specifics
- Optimistic framing that glosses over competitive threats
