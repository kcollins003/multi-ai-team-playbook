## Brand

**Handle: @FormFather**
- Decided: March 2026
- Platforms secured: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube
- Reasoning: "Form" is the direct English translation of kata. 
  "Father" carries the complete paternal identity. Alliterative, 
  Anglo, earned. ForgedFather (the original concept) was taken by 
  a 122K account in the same niche.

**Personal Instagram: @mrkevincollins**
- Kept separate from @FormFather
- Not a content channel — personal presence only

**Working bio:**
*Kata Man. Appointed by God. Entrusted with a lineage. Father of 
sons. The 10,000 is underway.*
- Decided: March 2026
- Master's name not yet public — bio reflects lineage without naming him

## Platform Strategy

**Primary: Instagram**
- Home base for all early content
- Decided: March 2026

**Parallel: TikTok**
- Cross-post all Instagram content from day one
- No TikTok-exclusive content until Instagram has momentum

**Deferred: YouTube**
- Long-form documentary potential (The 10,000 series)
- Do not activate until meaningful Instagram/TikTok momentum exists

## Content

**Three pillars:**
1. The Kata Journey — raw documentation of The 10,000
2. The Transformation — honest physical and spiritual check-ins
3. The Warrior Dad Perspective — first-person pieces on fatherhood, 
   discipline, faith, masculine virtue, legacy

**The 10,000:**
- 500 reps of each of 20 Shotokan kata forms in sequence
- Heian Shodan complete as of launch
- Document every kata completion — each is a milestone

## Narrative Sequencing

**Tell early:**
- The self-pity thread — most universal, most competitive advantage
- The Army story — own it before anyone else frames it; end on 16:11

**Tell when audience relationship is established:**
- His father's death — the tent in the garage
- His grandfather's hospice recovery

**Tell only when audience is ready to hold something sacred:**
- The Paradise Fire — testimony, not content play
- The bankruptcy and reset — matter-of-fact, no shame, on his terms

**Planned milestone moment — do not spend casually:**
- The lineage reveal (John Wilkes) — tied to major follower 
  milestone, completion of The 10,000, or birth of Daniel

## Monetization

**Decision: Audience first. No premature product structures.**
- Decided: March 2026
- Reasoning: Build the audience, let it reveal what it wants to 
  pay for. Likely future products: self-pity-to-transformation 
  framework, Warrior Dad philosophy as teachable system, kata/
  martial arts guidance.
